  • Perfection

    As a perfectionist, I have an eye for detail and handle every single project with care. I take into account UI Design as an important asset in web development since a correct and clear presentation of information will make the actual content of a website stand out

  • Responsivity

    I believe in a smart and smooth responsive design that embraces all web browsers and works well on all devices. Good responsiveness, as well as an easily understandable design, makes a website more interactive and enhances the user experience

  • Liveliness

    User interface design should not only be user-friendly and appealing to the eye but also needs to be lively. Animations bring a website alive and make its content more salient. I use React, vanilla Javascript and JQueryto intensify the interactivity and smoothing the flow of the page.

  • Creativity

    Solving issues requires a lot of creativity, which every web developer needs. Problem-solving skills and out of the box ideas make a website both visually attractive and prevent bugs from happening. Testing and debugging is thus part of the design process

About me

  • My story

    My name is Gregorio Gallo. I was born and raised in Prato (Italy, Tuscany) and moved to Florence to start my career at the age of nineteen. A few years later and many experiences richer, I decided that it was time for a next step. I did not hesitate a moment, packed my suitcase and move to Amsterdam, ready for a new adventure abroad. With hindsight, this has been one of the best decisions of my life.

  • Now

    As a Supply Chain Specialist, I work for one of the greatest international companies in the treatment of cancer with cell therapy. My working experiences have taught me to work in an international team, to communicate professionally and transparently and to deliver the best results on time. After more than a year of working in the Netherlands, I am ready for a new experience in another field.

  • My goal

    What started as a hobby, turned out into a great passion: web development. In the last months, my free time has been devoted to learning new programming languages and to growing as a web designer. Now that I have learned the main languages (HTML, CSS, JavaScript and a few of its libraries), I am ready to start working as a web developer and learn even more.
